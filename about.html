OUR STORY

Why We Started

Daily Health Tips was born from a simple observation: millions of people worldwide are searching for natural, holistic approaches to health, yet the wisdom of Traditional Chinese Medicine remains largely inaccessible to non-Chinese speakers.

Our team of wellness enthusiasts and Traditional Chinese Medicine practitioners came together with a shared vision—to translate thousands of years of Eastern health wisdom into practical, easy-to-understand guidance for modern life.
