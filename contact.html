Can you provide medical advice?

No. We are not healthcare providers and cannot diagnose, treat, or prescribe medical treatments. Our content is for informational purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.

How can I request new content?

Use the contact form above and select "Content Request" as the subject. Tell us what topics, exercises, or meal plans you'd like us to cover. We review all suggestions and prioritize based on audience interest.
