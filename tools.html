About BMI

BMI is a useful screening tool, but it doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, or body composition. Athletes may have high BMI but low body fat.

??

Calorie Needs

Your daily calorie needs depend on age, gender, weight, height, and activity level. These calculations provide estimates; individual needs may vary.

??

Hydration

Water needs vary based on activity, climate, and individual factors. These recommendations are general guidelines; listen to your body's thirst signals.

Sleep Cycles

Most adults need 7-9 hours of sleep. The calculator helps you wake at the end of a sleep cycle when you'll feel most refreshed.
