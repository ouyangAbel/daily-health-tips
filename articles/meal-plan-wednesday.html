Wednesday - Cleansing Day

📅 June 17, 2026 • Week of June 15–21 • Focus: Liver Detox
Advertisement Space (728x90)

Wednesday is Cleansing Day, dedicated to liver detoxification. The liver is the body's primary detox organ in both TCM and Western medicine. Bitter and green foods, citrus, and leafy greens all support the liver's natural cleansing processes.

Main Meal Plan

🌅 Breakfast: Green Smoothie with Spirulina and Lemon

Ingredients: 1 banana, handful of spinach, 1 tsp spirulina, juice of 1 lemon, 1 cup almond milk, 1 tbsp flax seeds

Method: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Drink immediately for maximum nutrient retention.

TCM Notes: Green foods support the liver. Spirulina is rich in chlorophyll which helps cleanse the blood. Lemon stimulates bile production and liver enzyme activity.

🥗 Lunch: Large Salad with Bitter Greens and Sprouts

Ingredients: Mixed bitter greens (dandelion, radicchio, endive), alfalfa sprouts, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, pumpkin seeds, olive oil and apple cider vinegar dressing

Method: Toss greens with sprouts, sliced cucumber, and halved cherry tomatoes. Top with pumpkin seeds. Dress with olive oil and apple cider vinegar.

TCM Notes: Bitter foods drain liver heat and promote detoxification. Sprouts are full of enzymes that aid digestion. The sour vinegar dressing supports the liver's storage function.

🐟 Dinner: Steamed Fish with Dill and Cucumber

Ingredients: 150g white fish fillet (cod or sea bass), fresh dill, cucumber slices, ginger, scallions, light soy sauce

Method: Place fish on a plate with ginger and scallions. Top with cucumber slices and dill. Steam for 10-12 minutes until fish flakes easily. Drizzle with soy sauce.

TCM Notes: Steaming preserves nutrients and is easy to digest. Fish provides lean protein without burdening the liver. Cucumber is cooling and supports the liver's cooling function.

Alternative Meal Plan

🔄 Alternative Breakfast: Dandelion Root Tea with Oatmeal

Brew dandelion root tea (a traditional liver tonic) and use it as the liquid for cooking oatmeal. Top with sliced pear and a drizzle of honey. Dandelion is one of TCM's most important liver-cleansing herbs.

🔄 Alternative Lunch: Chrysanthemum and Goji Green Tea Salad

Steep chrysanthemum flowers and goji berries in hot water for 5 minutes. Use this tea as a warm salad dressing over mixed greens, tofu, and edamame. Chrysanthemum clears liver heat and brightens the eyes.

🔄 Alternative Dinner: Artichoke and Lemon Soup

Simmer artichoke hearts with onion, garlic, lemon juice, and vegetable broth until soft. Blend until smooth. Artichokes are one of the best liver-protective foods, containing cynarin which stimulates bile production.

"The liver governs the free flow of Qi. When the liver is happy, energy flows smoothly throughout the body." — Traditional Chinese Medicine principle
