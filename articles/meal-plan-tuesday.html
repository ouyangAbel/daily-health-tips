Tuesday - Nourishing Day

📅 June 16, 2026 • Week of June 15–21 • Focus: Kidney & Liver
Advertisement Space (728x90)

Tuesday focuses on nourishing the kidneys and liver. In TCM, the kidneys store our constitutional energy (先天之精) while the liver ensures smooth flow of Qi throughout the body. Black and dark-colored foods nourish the kidneys, while sour and green foods support the liver.

Main Meal Plan

🌅 Breakfast: Black Sesame Porridge with Walnuts

Ingredients: ½ cup black sesame seeds, 1 cup rice, 6 cups water, 8 walnuts, honey to taste

Method: Grind black sesame seeds into a powder. Cook rice in water until soft. Stir in sesame powder and chopped walnuts. Cook 5 more minutes. Sweeten with honey.

TCM Notes: Black sesame is one of the premier kidney-nourishing foods in TCM. Walnuts are also kidney tonics and nourish the brain. This porridge is especially beneficial for those with graying hair, tinnitus, or lower back weakness.

🥗 Lunch: Grilled Salmon with Leafy Greens and Quinoa

Ingredients: 150g salmon fillet, 1 cup quinoa, 2 cups mixed leafy greens (spinach, arugula), lemon, olive oil, herbs

Method: Cook quinoa. Season salmon with herbs and grill 4 minutes per side. Toss greens with lemon and olive oil. Serve salmon over quinoa with greens on the side.

TCM Notes: Salmon nourishes the liver blood. Leafy greens are green (liver's color) and slightly bitter, supporting liver detoxification. Quinoa is a complete protein that's easy to digest.

🍜 Dinner: Bone Broth Soup with Root Vegetables

Ingredients: 2 lbs beef or chicken bones, carrots, daikon radish, ginger, scallions, goji berries, salt

Method: Blanch bones, then simmer in fresh water for 3-4 hours (or use a pressure cooker for 1 hour). Add root vegetables in the last 30 minutes. Add goji berries in the last 10 minutes. Season with salt.

TCM Notes: Bone broth is deeply nourishing to the kidneys and builds blood. Goji berries are a famous kidney and liver tonic. Root vegetables grow downward, grounding our energy.

Alternative Meal Plan

🔄 Alternative Breakfast: Goji Berry and Black Bean Porridge

Cook black beans until soft, then mash slightly and cook with rice porridge. Add a handful of goji berries in the last 5 minutes. Both black beans and goji berries are kidney-nourishing foods that are easy to prepare.

🔄 Alternative Lunch: Liver-Friendly Buddha Bowl

Roasted beets, steamed broccoli, avocado, edamame, and brown rice with a tahini dressing. Beets are a blood builder that supports the liver, while broccoli contains compounds that aid liver detoxification.

🔄 Alternative Dinner: Black Bean and Mushroom Soup

Simmer black beans with shiitake mushrooms, ginger, and garlic for a hearty, kidney-nourishing soup. Shiitake mushrooms boost immunity and support the kidney-adrenal axis. Ready in 30 minutes.

"The kidneys are the root of life. They store the essence from which all energy flows. To nourish the kidneys is to nourish life itself." — Huangdi Neijing (Yellow Emperor's Classic of Internal Medicine)
