Morning Sunlight for Better Sleep

📅 June 12, 2026 • Sleep Health
Advertisement Space (728x90)

Exposing yourself to natural sunlight within 30 minutes of waking helps regulate your circadian rhythm. This ancient practice aligns with the Chinese concept of following nature's rhythms.

Traditional Chinese Medicine teaches that Yang energy rises with the sun. By greeting the morning light, you activate your body's natural energy cycles.

Morning Sunlight Benefits

  • Regulates melatonin production
  • Boosts vitamin D synthesis
  • Improves mood and energy
  • Enhances nighttime sleep quality

Tip: Spend 10-15 minutes outside or near a window shortly after waking. No sunglasses needed—just let the light reach your eyes naturally.

"Rise with the sun, and you will set with contentment." — Traditional Chinese Proverb
