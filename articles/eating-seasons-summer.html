Eating with the Seasons: Summer Edition

?? June 9, 2026 ? Nutrition
Advertisement Space (728x90)

Summer calls for cooling foods to balance the body's internal heat. In Traditional Chinese Medicine, this follows the principle of harmony with nature's cycles.

During hot months, our digestive system naturally weakens. Light, cooling foods help maintain balance without overtaxing digestion.

Best Summer Foods

  • Watermelon: Natural hydrator, clears heat
  • Cucumber: Cooling, hydrating, low calorie
  • Mint: Refreshing, aids digestion
  • Bitter melon: Clears heat, supports liver
  • Green tea: Antioxidant, cooling properties

Avoid: Heavy, greasy foods and excessive spicy dishes during summer months.
