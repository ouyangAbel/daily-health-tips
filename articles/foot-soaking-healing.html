Foot Soaking: A Traditional Healing Practice

📏 June 10, 2026 ? TCM Remedies

Soaking your feet in warm water before bed promotes circulation, relaxation, and better sleep. This practice has been used in Chinese households for centuries.

The feet contain over 60 acupressure points connected to various organs. Warm water stimulates these points, promoting healing throughout the body.
